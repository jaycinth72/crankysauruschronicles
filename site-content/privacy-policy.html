🦖 The Crankysaurus Chronicles Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 August 2025

At The Crankysaurus Chronicles (TCC), we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our blog about prehistoric productivity and ROAR goals.

What information do we collect?

We collect minimal information from you:

  • Email subscriptions: If you sign up for our newsletter, we collect your email address
  • Ask Rexie submissions: When submitting questions to our advice column, we collect your email and any information you choose to share
  • Comments: We use CommentBox.io for comments, which has its own privacy policy
  • Website analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our site

How do we use your information?

Any information we collect is used for:

  • Sending you blog updates and prehistoric productivity wisdom (if you subscribe)
  • Responding to your Ask Rexie questions
  • Improving our website and content
  • Complying with legal requirements

We will never sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and tracking

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyse website traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • CommentBox.io: For blog comments
  • Email service provider: For newsletter subscriptions (when implemented)
  • Web hosting: For website operation

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

Data security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Ask questions about how we use your data

Children's privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

International users

This website is operated from New Zealand. If you are visiting from other regions with different privacy laws, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in New Zealand.
